TL;DW (YouTube Learning Aid)

This article presents TL;DW, an AI-powered tool designed to help students efficiently digest long-form YouTube educational videos. By leveraging large language models, the app generates concise summaries, key topics, interactive quizzes, and related content — all from a single video link. TL;DW streamlines learning by turning hours of content into focused, actionable insights.
